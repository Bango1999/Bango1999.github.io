EVGA GTX 1080 FTW Hybrid GPU

SEALED-BOX / SCARCE / VR-READY / 8GB GDDR5X PCIE 3.0

Up for bidding today is one of the very best consumer grade graphics cards available. The EVGA GTX 1080 FTW Hybrid is as beautiful as it is powerful, and as efficient as it is quiet.

EVGA's FTW (For The Win) series of graphics cards comes factory overclocked. This one is set to a 1721MHZ base and 1860MHZ boost clock, with 2560 CUDA cores, 8192MB of GDDR5X memory. The hybrid, named so because it employs a hybrid cooling system, part water pump and 120mm radiator block with 1x 120mm fan to cool the GPU while a blower style fan keeps air running directly over the VRAM, and across the rest of the PCB, can run even the most demanding games at superior framerates to standard blower/dual fan cards of the same specs.

This card is 100% VR-ready, and will simply smoke 99.9% of other consumer graphics cards out there right now.

Comes with marvels and baubels such as:

  • Radiator mounting screws (x4)
  • Fan preassembled to radiator
  • Dual 6-pin to single 8-pin power cable adapters (x2)
  • Black envelope with manual, stickers, an EVGA poster, and other product papers.

NOTE: No SLI Bridge included.
